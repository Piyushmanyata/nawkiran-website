# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Procurement owners, bottlers, fillers, packaging converters, and distributors who need PET preforms matched to a neck finish, gram weight, use case, and reliable supply window. They are often scanning on mobile while comparing specs or asking for a quick quote.

## Product Purpose

The site presents Nawkiran Polyplast as a Kolkata-based PET preform manufacturer with a serious catalogue, visible manufacturing capacity, and a fast WhatsApp-led quote path. Success means buyers can identify the right preform family and spec without calling first.

## Brand Personality

Industrial, precise, direct. The interface should feel like a working manufacturer with real products and real dispatch history, not a generic template landing page.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS-style metric cards, decorative factory illustrations, clumsy product grids that force repeated scrolling, vague plastic bottle stock art, and oversized claims without catalogue detail.

## Design Principles

- Let the catalogue behave like a specification tool, not a brochure block.
- Show the physical product shape wherever a buyer is choosing a spec.
- Keep sales actions close to the selected neck and gram weight.
- Use numbers where they prove capability, but do not let raw counts crowd brand sections.
- Preserve the WhatsApp-led ordering model and avoid implying checkout.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible catalogue selection, readable mobile tap targets, visible focus states, and reduced-motion fallbacks for all animated sections.
